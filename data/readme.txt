## Data

All generated data is saved in this directory.

NextPolish:
polishing of the previously published reference genome

repeatmasking:
masking of repetitive elements in the polished reference genome

braker2:
computational annotation using BRAKER2

braker_analysis:
analysis of BRAKER2 output and classification into different support subsets

isoseq:
assembly of full-length transcript sequencing data

reannotation_correction:
merge of computational annotation and full-length transcript sequencing data, deduplication and renaming of annotated genes

annotation_analysis:
identification of flavonoid and betalain pathway genes and MYB transcription factor genes
